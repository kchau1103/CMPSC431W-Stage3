We are starstruck

STARSTRUCK IS THE STAGE WHERE PERFORMERS OF ALL AGES AND LEVELS, ACROSS THE GLOBE SHOWCASE THEIR TALENT.

Every year, Studio Owners, Teachers, Performers, and their Families and Friends are welcomed at upwards of 80 creative and energy packed competitions in cities across the globe.

we celebrate the art of dance and competition. Our event is designed for dancers of all ages and levels to showcase their talents, learn, and grow.

Here's what you need to know about our competition:


Registration Costs

Each dance piece registration is priced at $100. This fee covers the administrative costs, venue, and awards.

Age Groups

  • Mini: 8-10 years
  • Junior: 11-12 years
  • Teen: 13-15 years
  • Senior: 16-19 years

Age groups are determined by the average age of the dancers in each piece.


Adjudication Ranges

Dancers are judged by a panel of experienced judges, and scores are awarded out of 100. The final score determines the award category:

  • Platinum: 291-300 points
  • High Gold: 282-290.9 points
  • Gold: 273-281.9 points
  • Silver: 264-272.9 points
  • Bronze: Below 264 points

Dance Styles

We welcome a variety of dance styles at our competition, including but not limited to:

  • Ballet
  • Jazz
  • Hip-Hop
  • Contemporary
  • Tap
  • Lyrical
  • Musical Theatre
  • Folk

Starstruck Dance Competition is committed to providing a platform for dancers to shine and express their passion for dance. We look forward to seeing the incredible talent and hard work at our next event!
